Ethernet City

The way that I see the city to be an infrastructure is the roads and paths that connect each city to city, suburb to suburb, building to building like the cables of ethernet. The similarities continue when there is a lot of traffic (car or internet), the speed slows down with more people using it so I wanted to capture this to show how similar they are. The images below are taken from google maps with the 'traffic' option on and then some post production and the results make the city of Wellington look like connection of the internet. As you can see on the images there is a key on the bottom which tells you how fast or slow the traffic is moving which creates a cool looking image which still displays the relevant information.
